STEP 1: Open Control Panel.	

STEP 2: Click on Network & Security center.

STEP 3: Click on Set up a new connection or network.

STEP 4: Select Connect to a Workplace and click on next.

STEP 5: Click on Use my Internet connection (VPN).

STEP 6: Add Ip Address in Internet Address and click on Create.

Step 7: In Left Panel of Network and Sharing Center window click on Change Adapter settings.

STEP 8: In Network Connection window, You can see your created VPN connection.

STEP 9: Left click on VPN Connection and Click on Properties.

STEP 10: In properties window, Go to Security tab and click on Advanced Settings.

STEP 11: Check the Advanced Properties of VPN Connections.
